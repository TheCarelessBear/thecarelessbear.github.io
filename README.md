# mar.gins

**Know your numbers. Own your kitchen.**

A free, browser-based food cost calculator for independent restaurants, bakeries, pizza shops, sandwich spots, caterers, and any food business that wants to understand their real numbers.

---

## What it does

- **Ingredient library** — Enter every ingredient you buy, the pack size, unit, and what you paid. The app calculates your cost-per-gram/ml/unit automatically.
- **Recipe builder** — Build your recipes from your ingredient library. See your exact cost per serving instantly.
- **Menu & pricing** — Set your menu prices and see your food cost %, gross profit per plate, and margin health at a glance. Flags items that are hurting your bottom line.
- **Cost simulator** — Drag a slider to simulate what happens to your margins if ingredient costs go up 10%, 20%, 50%. See the impact on every recipe instantly.
- **Waste tracker** — Log waste events by ingredient and reason. See in real dollars how much spoilage, over-prepping, and accidents are costing you each month.

---

## How to use it

No sign-up required. No download. Open the link in any browser on any device.

Your data saves automatically to your browser's local storage. It will still be there when you come back.
